Engineering Technology

Bachelor of Science (BSET)
120
Credit Hours
$62K
Median Starting Salary
2–4 yrs
Flexible Pathways

NAU's Engineering Technology program emphasizes the practical application of engineering principles to real-world problems. Unlike traditional engineering degrees, this program focuses on applied engineering practices and technical problem-solving. Two emphasis tracks are available: General Engineering and Semiconductor Fabrication. Flexible completion pathways include a 4-year standalone option, a 2-year completion track for community college transfers, and the 90-30 option for Pima CC students.
