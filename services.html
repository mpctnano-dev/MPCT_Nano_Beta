Laser Structuring (LPKF)

Leverage the ultra-short pulse LPKF ProtoLaser R4 for "cold" laser ablation without thermal damage. Ideal for structuring delicate transparent films, precise cutting of hardened glass, and creating specialized RF/Microwave circuits with micron-level accuracy.

  • Rapid Microfluidic Prototyping
