Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats do you accept?

We accept STL and 3MF files only. Most CAD programs (Fusion 360, SolidWorks, Blender, TinkerCAD) can export in these formats.

How much does it cost?

Pricing is quote-based and depends on material, size, and print time. You'll receive a detailed estimate before any work begins — no surprises.

How long does printing take?

Most jobs are completed within 3–5 business days. Larger or more complex prints may take longer — we'll include a timeline in your quote.

Can I watch my print being made?

We don't currently offer live viewing, but you'll receive status updates by email at every stage of the process.

What if my print fails?

If a print fails due to a machine error or issue on our end, we'll reprint it at no extra cost. If the failure is caused by a design issue, we'll contact you to discuss adjustments.
