B-2 Atomic Force Microscope

The AFM Workshop B-2 is a versatile platform designed for both advanced research and educational training. It provides high-resolution 3D topographical imaging and supports multiple scanning modes for characterizing nanomaterials.

System Capabilities

The B-2 AFM offers a robust balance of performance and accessibility. It utilizes a linearized piezoelectric scanner to ensure accurate measurements at the nanoscale.

Designed for multi-user environments, it features an open architecture that allows for easy probe exchange and modified sample staging, making it ideal for standard wafers as well as irregular research samples.

Operating Modes

Vibrating Mode

Also known as Tapping Mode. Ideal for soft or fragile samples to minimize damage.

Contact Mode

Provides high-resolution topography for hard, stable surfaces.

LFM / Phase

Lateral Force Microscopy measures friction; Phase imaging detects composition.

Education

Transparent design allows students to visualize the optical lever path.
