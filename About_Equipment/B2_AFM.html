B-2 Atomic Force Microscope

The AFM Workshop B-2 is a versatile atomic force microscope designed for both advanced research and educational training. It provides high-resolution 3D topographical imaging and supports multiple scanning modes, making it an essential tool for characterizing nanomaterials.

Book Equipment View Specs

Capabilities & Modes

  • Vibrating (Tapping) Mode: Ideal for soft or fragile samples.
  • Contact Mode: High-resolution topography for hard surfaces.
  • Phase Imaging: Detect variations in surface composition.
  • Lateral Force Microscopy (LFM): Measures surface friction.

Technical Specifications

Z Scan Range 17 µm
