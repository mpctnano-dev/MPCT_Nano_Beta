System Capabilities

The Bambu Lab H2D is a dual-nozzle FDM/FFF printer designed for multi-material and multi-color printing. It uses a full-featured Bambu control system and supports optional laser and cutting modules when installed.

It is intended for functional prototypes, end-use parts, and mid-size models, with support for engineering filaments depending on configuration.

Operating Modes

Single-Extrusion Printing

Full build area printing with one nozzle for single-material jobs.

Dual-Extrusion Printing

Two nozzles for multi-material or multi-color parts.

AMS-Assisted Printing

Automatic material handling with AMS 2 Pro or AMS HT systems.

Laser and Cutting Modules (Optional)

Optional laser engraving/cutting and blade or pen modules when installed.
