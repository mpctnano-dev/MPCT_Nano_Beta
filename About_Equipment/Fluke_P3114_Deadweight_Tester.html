System Capabilities

The P3114 is part of Fluke's P3100 series hydraulic deadweight testers. It generates precise pressure using calibrated mass and piston-cylinder assemblies, suitable for high-accuracy calibration.

The system supports traceable calibration for gauges, transducers, and pressure instruments.

Operating Modes

Deadweight Pressure

Generate pressure by applying calibrated weights.

Piston Rotation

Maintain stability and reduce friction for accuracy.

Gauge Calibration

Compare DUT readings to generated pressure.

Traceable Results

Support calibration traceability requirements.
