System Capabilities

The VHX-7000 provides high-resolution digital microscopy for inspection and measurement tasks. Its 4K output and high-resolution sensor support clear imaging of microstructures, surfaces, and defects.

The system is suited for routine inspection, documentation, and measurement workflows in materials and device evaluation.

Operating Modes

High-Magnification Imaging

Detailed observation of micro-features and surface textures.

Wide-Area Inspection

Capture larger fields of view for overview and context.

Measurement Tools

On-screen measurement and annotation for reports.

Documentation

High-resolution image capture and export for records.
