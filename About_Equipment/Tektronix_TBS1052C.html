Tektronix TBS1052C Digital Oscilloscope

A 2-channel digital storage oscilloscope for time-domain signal analysis. The TBS1052C provides 50 MHz bandwidth with 1 GS/s sampling for general lab measurements and troubleshooting.

System Capabilities

The TBS1052C is a general-purpose digital oscilloscope for waveform visualization and measurement. It supports time-domain analysis of analog and mixed-frequency signals in lab and instructional settings.

Its 2-channel configuration enables comparison of input and output signals and timing analysis.

Operating Modes

Time-Domain Viewing

Real-time visualization of voltage vs. time.

Triggering

Stable waveform capture with standard trigger modes.

Measurements

Frequency, amplitude, and timing measurements.

Data Capture

Capture and export waveforms for reports.
