System Capabilities

The Model 160 provides controlled mechanical grinding for TEM specimen preparation. It is designed for pre-thinning to create a flat, uniform section prior to dimpling or ion milling.

Specimens are securely mounted to grinding platens, enabling repeatable thinning with tight thickness control.

Operating Modes

Coarse Grinding

Rapid material removal for initial pre-thinning.

Fine Grinding

Controlled thinning to achieve uniform thickness.

Thickness Control

Micrometer-based control for repeatable results.

Platen Transfer

Move specimens directly to a dimpling grinder.
