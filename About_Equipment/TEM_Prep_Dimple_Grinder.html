Sample Prep Dimpling

Fischione Model 200 TEM Prep Dimple Grinder

A dimpling grinder for TEM specimen preparation. It thins the center of 3 mm discs to just a few microns using a rotating wheel and abrasive slurry, preparing samples for final ion milling.

System Capabilities

The Model 200 thins the center of a disk specimen to create a dimple suitable for final thinning. It can be used for single- or double-sided dimpling with precise depth control.

The dimpled region is typically finished by ion milling to achieve electron transparency.

Operating Modes

Single-Sided Dimpling

Thin one side to form a central dimple.

Double-Sided Dimpling

Optional for symmetric thinning and reduced stress.

Depth Control

Fine control to stop at target thickness.

Final Ion Milling

Prepare for electron-transparent finishing.
