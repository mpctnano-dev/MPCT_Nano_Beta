Microtrac BELPREP VAC Series

A vacuum degassing and pretreatment system for adsorption analysis. It removes moisture and gases from samples to ensure accurate surface area and pore measurements.

System Capabilities

The BELPREP VAC series prepares samples for adsorption analysis by removing moisture and adsorbed gases under vacuum or inert gas flow. It supports independent heating of multiple samples.

Pretreatment improves accuracy for BET and pore size measurements by establishing a reliable dry mass before analysis.

Operating Modes

Vacuum Heating

Standard degassing under vacuum to remove moisture and gases.

Flow Heating

Inert gas purge for samples sensitive to high vacuum.

Independent Ports

Simultaneous pretreatment of 3 or 6 samples.

Safety Zone

Radiation zone for controlled cooling after heating.

Technical Specifications

Models VAC II (3 ports) or VAC III (6 ports)

Pressure Limit Do not exceed 0.2 MPa (G)
Vacuum Requirement External rotary pump (>= 20 L/min)
Heating Programs Programmable with multi-step profiles (VAC III)
Recommended Gas Inert purge gas such as nitrogen
