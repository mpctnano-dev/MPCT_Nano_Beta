System Capabilities

The Keysight E5063A ENA supports precision S-parameter measurements for filters, cables, antennas, and matching networks. It provides a wide frequency range with strong dynamic range and low trace noise for accurate RF characterization.

Multiple calibration methods are supported (SOLT, TRL, ECal, and more), along with optional time-domain analysis, mixed-mode S-parameters, and fixture de-embedding for advanced workflows.

Operating Modes

Frequency Sweeps

Linear, log, and segmented sweeps for standard RF characterization.

S-Parameters

2-port measurements of S11, S21, S12, and S22.

Time-Domain (Option)

Reflectometry and response analysis for locating discontinuities.

Calibration & De-Embedding

SOLT, TRL, ECal, and adapter removal/insertion.
