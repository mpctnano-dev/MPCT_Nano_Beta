System Capabilities

The Smart Robot Workcell is an educational training system built around a FANUC LR Mate 200iD/4S robot for teaching robot operation, programming, and troubleshooting in a realistic automated cell.

Workcell configurations vary, but training systems can include robot material handling, palletizing logistics, and cell-level safety and control features.

Operating Modes

Pick and Place

High-speed handling and part transfer using the 6-axis robot.

Machine Tending

Robot operation for part loading and unloading in automated cells.

Logistics / Palletizing (Config Dependent)

Workcell configurations may include conveyors, pallets, and sorting tasks.

Safety and Interlocks

Training on safety devices and interlocked cell operation.
