System Capabilities

The 87-MS7 Inventory Storage Station is a programmable parts storage workstation for teaching PLC-based sequencing, pneumatic pick-and-place storage, and automated inventory handling.

It includes a programmable position pneumatic robot, 4-channel storage module, parts presentation module, and operator station integrated with sensors and electro-pneumatic control.

Operating Modes

Programmable Parts Storage

Stores and retrieves parts using the 4-channel storage module.

Pneumatic Pick and Place Storage

Pneumatic robot transfers assemblies between presentation and storage.

Sensor-Based Control

Infrared and optical sensors provide position and part feedback.

PLC Manual/Auto/Reset

PLC programs manage manual, automatic, and reset station functions.
