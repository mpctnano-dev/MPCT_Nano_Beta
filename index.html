Welcome to the NAU Microelectronics Processing and Characterization Testing Lab (MPaCT). We are the premier open-access research hub in Northern Arizona, dedicated to transforming academic theory into industrial reality.

As a central pillar of Arizona’s emerging semiconductor corridor, we provide state-of-the-art metrology, lithography, deposition, and characterization tools. Our mission is to cultivate a transdisciplinary ecosystem where students, faculty, and industry partners collaborate to push the boundaries of materials science and device engineering.

As a detailed shared-use facility, we are open to faculty and private companies nationwide. We provide access to our advanced equipment and expert staff to support diverse research and development needs across the country.
